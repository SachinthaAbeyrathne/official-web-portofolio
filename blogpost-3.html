"Words have the Power to Heal.So Choose it Wisely"

POSTED ON MAY 16, 2020

I believe in the power of words. Many people speak before they think, but words can make you, break you and they can heal your soul. They can damage you forever. So always try to use positive words in life wherever you go.

As an example when something bad happens you called it "Adversity" and I call it "Opportunity". And at the same time, people say "Weakness" but I call it "Strength."

There are some incidents that happen in your life and those incidents are so strong to change your DNA.

Those incidents or accidents are so strong that they can break you physically, they deform your body, but they transform your soul. Those incidents that break you and deform you will mold you into the best version of you.
